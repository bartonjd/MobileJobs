Ext.ux.DragSelect

Ever wanted more control over cursor selection? Well Now you can have all the control you want. Ext.ux.DragSelect allows you to plug in a startDrag function, onDrag function and endDrag function. Though still in it's initial stages of development I appreciate any comments or suggestions. Please contact me on the http://extjs.com/forum/ by sending a message or posting to my thread.
